About Satori

Satori (悟り) is a Japanese Buddhist term for awakening, "comprehension; understanding".

In the Zen Buddhist tradition, satori refers to the experience of seeing into one's true nature", satori is commonly translated as enlightenment,

The satori platform and products derived from it allow deeper understanding and visualization of the true nature of phenomena, products and principles by using immersive 3D/AR/VR technologies, This suite of products have been built to solve problems faced in sales and marketing, industry 4.0, process control, industrial training and education.
