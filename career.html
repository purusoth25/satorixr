Senior Augmented Reality iOS Developer

As a Senior Augmented Reality developer, you will be a technical leader converting ideas into design and high performance experiences using Unity or iOS ARKit and you will have the opportunity to create next generation solutions for enterprises giving them a competitive edge in the market as well as in internal processes.

We are a thought-leader and closely watched company, so we’re looking for someone who will take complete ownership of leading our AR initiatives and is also willing to work in the forefront of the augmented reality industry.

    Responsibilities:
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams of engineers, designers, artists, and product managers as we define and deliver customer experiences
  • Participate in technical design and code reviews
  • Handle end-to-end delivery of a complex iOS project that involves ARKit + Unity(or others)
  • Build high quality, reliable, scalable, high-performance, gamified visualization systems
  • Mentoring junior developers. As we grow, you will likely work with new hires and also contracting resources so it’s good if you are able to break up a complicated engineering task into sections and delegate some aspects
Additional Skills:
  • Knowledge of AR/3D WebGL Technologies such as Three.js, and Babylon.js
  • Experience working with artists and creators
Work Location:
  • Preferred Velachery, Chennai (Accommodation can be provided) or Basavanagudi, Bangalore
  • Notice Period: 30 days or less
    Required Skills:
  • Proficiency in systems programming language, such as Objective-C, Swift, etc.
  • Extensive experience using ARKit, experience with iOS frameworks like ARKit, UIKit, AVFoundation, Core Motion, CloudKit, SiriKit, StoreKit etc is a bonus
  • Some experience working with APIs, familiarization with sending authorization tokens and pulling data
  • Experience with at least one 3d application development engine (Unreal, Unity3D, etc.)
  • Good knowledge and use of design patterns and architecture
  • Sound knowledge in technologies such as 2D and 3D math, physics, rendering, animation
  • Experience with Immersive projects using Camera, Lights, Textures, Animations and shaders
