About Satori

Satori (悟り) is a Japanese Buddhist term, commonly translated as enlightenment and refers to the experience of seeing into one's true nature.

The satori platform and products derived from it allows deeper understanding and visualization of phenomena, products and principles using immersive 3D/XR/Digital Twin technologies.

Who we are

Vibromech Advanced Technologies LLP, the technology division of the vibromech group (www.vibromech.com), is founded by seasoned technocrat entrepreneurs with decades of experience with OEM manufacturing of high tech and proprietary products, engineering design and enterprise software development.

Our pedigree

Vibromech, established in 1970, is a pioneer in India with its products and technologies.

Vibromech is known and awarded for Innovation and is associated with the best in the world - customers, partners, universities and talent.

Vision

The Satori vision is to achieve industry goals of reducing cost, increasing profitability and sustainability.

SatoriXR products and services using 3D/XR/industry 4.0 technologies have been instrumental in our clients’ digital transformation journeys.
