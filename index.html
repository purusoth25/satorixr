About Satori

Satori (悟り) is a Japanese Buddhist term, commonly translated as enlightenment and refers to the experience of seeing into one's true nature.

The satori platform and products derived from it allows deeper understanding and visualization of phenomena, products and principles using immersive 3D/XR/Digital Twin technologies. This industry 4.0 suite of products has been built to solve problems faced in sales and marketing, process control, industrial training and education.
